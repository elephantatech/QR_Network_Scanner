‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: "Could not open camera" error?
This is a macOS permission issue.
1. Click Allow when prompted.
2. RESTART the app completely.
3. Check System Settings > Privacy > Camera.
Q: Scanning but not detecting?
Move closer/further. Ensure good lighting. Avoid glare.
Q: Where are the logs?
Debug Log: ~/qr_network_debug.log
Crash Log: ~/qr_crash.log
